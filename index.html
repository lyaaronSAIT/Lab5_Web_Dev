Team FairShare Presents The FairShare App

Hi we are Team FairShare

Have you ever experienced troubles splitting the bill?

Some people are okay with splitting it evenly, some would rather pay for their portions only. It deffinetly can be quite challenging to please everyone! That is why we at Team FairShare created this moblie application to help relieve the stress and frustration from figuring out ways to split the bill.

We created this team and app in our Software Analysis and Design (CPRG 200 F) spring semester class. Please check out our fidelty sketches and leave any comments on what we can improve on!
